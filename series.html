Profile

In a world where superheroes embrace the darker side of their massive celebrity and fame, THE BOYS centers on a group of vigilantes known informally as “The Boys,” who set out to take down corrupt superheroes with no more than their blue collar grit and a willingness to fight dirty.

Series

The Office

The show documents the exploits of a paper supply company in Scranton, Pennsylvania. With an office including the likes of various peers, this series takes a look at the lives of its co-workers.

The Family Guy

Peter Griffin tries to do the right thing for his assorted family of two teenagers, a smart dog and a devilish baby. Despite his good intentions, Peter ends up making hilarious mistakes all the time

HIMYM

Ted Mosby, an architect, recounts to his children the events that led him to meet their mother. His journey is made more eventful by the presence of his friends Lily, Marshall, Robin and Barney

Brooklyn 99

Ray Holt, an eccentric commanding officer, and his diverse and quirky team of odd detectives solve crimes in Brooklyn, New York City.

Modern Family

Three modern-day families from California try to deal with their kids, quirky spouses and jobs in their own unique ways, often falling into hilarious situations.

The Simpsons

Features a satirical portrayal of middle-class American lifestyle. Caricatures certain facets of the American society, culture, television and many features of the human condition.

Daredevil

Blind lawyer Matt Murdock fights crime at night as the super-hero Daredevil while trying to balance his day job.

House MD

Dr. Gregory House, although a medical unconventional, is very skilled and experienced in leading a team of medical diagnostics.

The Walking Dead

In the wake of a zombie apocalypse, various survivors struggle to stay alive. As they search for safety and evade the undead, they are forced to grapple with rival groups and difficult choices.

Breaking Bad

Walter White, a chemistry teac, discovers that he has cancer and decides to get into the meth-making business to repay his medical debts. His priorities begin to change when he partners with Jesse.

True Detective

Louisiana State Police detectives Rust Cohle and Marty Hart as they investigate a string of murders, including one involving a prostitute in 1995.

Better Call Saul

Ex-con artist Jimmy McGill turns into a small-time attorney and goes through a series of trials and tragedies, as he transforms into his alter ego Saul Goodman, a morally challenged criminal lawyer

The Sopranos

Tony Soprano, an Italian-American mafia head based in New Jersey, struggles to manage his family and criminal life and confides his affairs to his psychiatrist Jennifer Melfi.

Game of Thrones

Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.
